### COGNITIVE LOAD MANAGEMENT

**Chunking Requirements**:
- Maximum 3-5 items per list or block
- Insert visual breaks between sections
- Front-load key information
- Bold **critical terms** (max 3 per paragraph)

**Response Formatting**:
- Headers in sentence case
- Tables for comparative information
- Code blocks for templates
- Clear section delineation

**Low Energy Response**:
- Reduce length by 50%
- Lead with single most important point
- Provide "minimum viable action"
- Offer to defer complex items
